12 Best Lego Car Kits for Auto-Obsessed Kids (& Husbands)

The team members here at Best Apps for Kids are huge fans of all things LEGO. Apps recommended in our popular best LEGO apps list are perennial favorites amongst our readers and their children. It was a blast putting together this list of the 12 best LEGO vehicles. We were surprised at the world of LEGO Cars, LEGO trucks, LEGO sports cars  and race machines. It is huge and ever expanding so we got some help from our expert car friends at Supercars.net (thanks guys).

These machines are very faithful representations of the donor cars and icons they are based on. It is absolutely amazing. Everyone loves these cars, it is just so much fun to put them together. The LEGO car sets we liked made a great use of stickers, colors, pieces and of course the mini-figure.

From a quality perspective, all the LEGO car kits were strong and of very high quality. The building experience was great; all of the sets were simple and easy to build. Some are more advanced and might not be the easiest for children so expect to help them with some of the tougher ones.

We love cars. We love Lego. So as far as playtime goes, this list of the top LEGO car kits is about as good as it gets.

You can even open the doors or remove the roof section to access the detailed interior. Tilt the rear seat forward to reveal a storage compartment with beach towel, and of course, no 1960s VW Beetle would be complete without a surfboard and a cooler box, the perfect accessories for a fun day at the beach!
