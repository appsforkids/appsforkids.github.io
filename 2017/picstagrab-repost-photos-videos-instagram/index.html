Review Summary:

Picstagrab – Repost Photos & Videos for Instagram is a handy utility that will simplify and automate the process of grabbing, captioning, and posting photos on your Instagram Account.

App Info

Price: Free
AppStore User Rating:

Overview

Picstagrab is a handy utility that allows users to grab, post and repost pictures from Instagram with or without captions or optional watermarks. The app does a quick search to see if the picture has already been used multiple times, and reminds you to make sure you have permission to use photos owned by others.

Features include:

  • Store list of favorite users and hashtags
  • Share captions, watermarks and more between iOS devices
  • Create and upload captions from your PC through Itunes
  • Easy access to favorites via “recently-viewed” section
  • Easy upload and management

Quality

Picstagrab is a high quality app that will be quite useful to users of Instagram. The app allows users to snag pictures off of the Instagram feed, caption and/or place a watermark, and post. Pull pictures from your camera roll, your feed, and more.  The app reminds users to be sure they have the right to use the photo before posting, and will check and notify users if a photo has been used before.  The one thing it could use is a more detailed tutorial…it can seem pretty bewildering for anyone who is not already an Instagram user.

Education

This app is not intended to be educational.

Entertainment

Instagram users will be thrilled with this app since it simplifies and automates the process of putting up pictures with optional captions and/or watermarks.  The app is easy to use and offers a wealth of creative options.  It definitely would make the job of putting content onto Instagram easier and less time-consuming, making it ideal for both business and personal use.

Value

Picstagrab is free on the App Store, making it a great value.  If you’re not sure if the app is what you need, you can easily try it out for a while with absolutely no risk.  There are optional in-app purchases to remove the various kinds of advertisements, ranging from ninety-nine cents to three dollars and ninety-nine cents.

Child Friendliness

This app is not really intended for children’s use. It has third-party advertisements and at least two unprotected in-app purchases (to remove ads), as well as unprotected ratings pop-ups.

  • YES 3rd party advertisements
  • YES unprotected in-app purchases
  • YES unprotected social media
  • YES unprotected ratings pop-ups
  • YES unprotected links and email
